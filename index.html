About Us

City Chemist: Your Trusted Pharmacy in Dehradun

Welcome to City Chemist, the premier destination for all your pharmaceutical needs in the heart of Dehradun. Established on October 24, 2014, we have grown to become the largest and most trusted pharmacy in the region, located at C-17, Nehru Colony. Our dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a vital part of the community.

A Premium Experience Like No Other

Step into City Chemist and experience a premium, mall-like atmosphere where every detail is thoughtfully arranged to meet your health and wellness needs. We take pride in offering a wide range of branded medicines and pharmaceutical products, making sure you can find everything you need in one convenient location.

Our store is designed with your comfort in mind. With neatly organized shelves, friendly staff, and a welcoming environment, shopping at City Chemist is a smooth and enjoyable experience. Whether you need prescription medications, over-the-counter remedies, or healthcare products, we have it all.

Unmatched Convenience and Care

At City Chemist, we prioritize your well-being and convenience above all else. We aim to create a friendly atmosphere where you can effortlessly find what you need and receive the support you deserve.

Understanding the importance of convenience, City Chemist offers free parking for all our customers. Our ample parking area, situated right next to the shop, allows you to visit us without any stress, enhancing your shopping experience.

Experience the difference at City Chemist - where your health is our priority, and your satisfaction is our mission.
