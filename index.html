About Us

City Chemist: Your Trusted Pharmacy in Dehradun

Welcome to City Chemist, the premier destination for all your pharmaceutical needs in the heart of Dehradun. Established on October 24, 2014, we have grown to become the largest and most trusted pharmacy in the region, located at C-17, Nehru Colony. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a cornerstone of the community.

A Premium Experience Like No Other

Step into City Chemist and immerse yourself in a premium, mall-like atmosphere where every detail is meticulously curated to cater to your health and wellness needs. We pride ourselves on offering an extensive selection of branded medicines and pharmaceutical products, ensuring you find everything you're looking for under one roof.

Our store is designed with your comfort in mind. With well-organized shelves, knowledgeable staff, and a welcoming environment, shopping at City Chemist is a hassle-free experience. Whether you're looking for prescription medications, over-the-counter remedies, or healthcare products, we have it all.

Unmatched Convenience and Care

At City Chemist, your well-being and convenience are our top priorities. We strive to create a welcoming environment where you can easily find what you need and receive the support you deserve.

Understanding the importance of convenience, City Chemist provides free parking for all our customers. Our spacious parking area, located beside the shop, ensures that you can visit us without any hassle, making your shopping experience even more enjoyable.

Experience the difference at City Chemist - where your health is our priority, and your satisfaction is our mission.
