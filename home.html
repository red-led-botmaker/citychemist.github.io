    City Chemist — Dehradun’s Trusted Pharmacy

    City Chemist is Dehradun’s largest pharmacy. We bring you a wide range of branded medicines, quality orthotics, baby care products, and everyday health essentials — all in one place.

    Located at C-16 & C-17, Nehru Colony, our store is designed to give you a clean, organized, and friendly shopping experience. Our team is always ready to help you find exactly what you need.

    We offer free parking and GST billing to make your visit smooth and convenient.
