About City Chemist

City Chemist is Dehradun’s largest, premium pharmacy offering branded medicines, cosmetics, and health essentials.

Founded in 2014, we have served over 50,000 customers with a focus on genuine products, fast service, and professional care. Our bright, organized store and licensed pharmacists ensure a modern, hassle-free experience.

We stock 35,000+ items including prescription medicines, supplements, skincare, baby care, and surgicals. Our team is dedicated to helping you find the right products and providing dosage guidance.

Located in the heart of Dehradun, we offer free parking and easy access. Whether you need a quick refill or expert advice, City Chemist is here to support your health and wellness journey.

Thank you for trusting City Chemist. We look forward to serving you!
