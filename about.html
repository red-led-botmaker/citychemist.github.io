City Chemist: Your Trusted Pharmacy in Dehradun‎

Serving 35,000+ Medicines, Cosmetics & Health Essentials‎ ‎

Located at C-17, Nehru Colony, City Chemist has proudly served the Dehradun community since October 24, 2014. Over the past decade, we’ve grown into the largest and most recognized pharmacy in the city, built on trust, transparency, and genuine care. Our journey started with a clear mission — to redefine what a modern-day pharmacy should be. Today, with over 35,000 medical and wellness products under one roof, we continue to deliver excellence every single day.

Our Mission & Vision‎ ‎

Our mission is simple but powerful — to enhance the health and happiness of every individual who walks through our doors. We are committed to offering:

• 100% genuine, branded medicines at fair prices
• Trusted wellness, surgical, and cosmetic products
• Personalized pharmaceutical guidance from licensed professionals

Our long-term vision is to be more than just a pharmacy — to become a dependable health partner in every household in Dehradun by combining professional service with innovation and convenience.

A Premium Pharmacy Experience‎

City Chemist isn't just a store — it’s an experience. As soon as you step inside, you'll notice the difference. Our pharmacy is designed to resemble a modern retail space, with a mall-like atmosphere that’s bright, clean, and inviting.

• Spacious and well-organized shelves grouped by category
• Air-conditioned interiors with relaxing ambient lighting
• Courteous and trained staff always ready to assist
• Clearly labeled counters for fast billing and quick checkout

From chronic prescriptions to everyday essentials, you’ll find everything in one hassle-free visit.

What We Offer

Our inventory covers a comprehensive spectrum of healthcare needs:

• Prescription and over-the-counter medicines from trusted national and international brands
• Health supplements and nutraceuticals for immunity, energy, and lifestyle support
• Personal care and cosmetic products including skin, hair, and hygiene essentials
• Baby care items, wellness devices, and healthcare kits
• Surgical supplies and homecare essentials

All items are verified for authenticity and stored under strict temperature-controlled conditions to ensure quality and safety.

Free Parking & Prime Location‎

We know how hectic city life can be — that’s why we make your visit effortless. Our pharmacy features:

• A dedicated, on-site free parking lot right beside the store
• Easy-to-access ground-floor location in a prominent area of Nehru Colony

Whether you’re driving in or walking by, City Chemist is convenient to reach, no matter the time or day.

Professionalism Meets Personal Touch

We believe healthcare is not just about products, but about people. That’s why we ensure every interaction is met with care, empathy, and guidance. Our certified pharmacists:

• Help you understand your prescriptions
• Provide dosage advice and drug interactions info
• Assist with medicine substitutes when needed
• Handle emergency needs with calm and speed

Our team treats every customer like family, ensuring you leave with peace of mind.

GST Billing & Digital Convenience‎ ‎

We offer full GST-compliant billing for all purchases — helping you keep medical records and avail health insurance reimbursements with ease. For tech-savvy users and working professionals, we also provide:

• Digital invoicing
• WhatsApp-based product inquiries and availability checks
• Pre-ordering and advance medicine booking
• Cashless payments and UPI support

At City Chemist, modern technology blends with traditional service to offer unmatched convenience.

A Name Dehradun Trusts

Over the years, City Chemist has become more than just a pharmacy — we’ve become a trusted part of the community. We’re proud to have helped thousands of families, senior citizens, and young parents through every phase of life. From first-aid to long-term care, we’re always by your side.
